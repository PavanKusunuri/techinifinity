## The Challenge

FinanceCore was running a sprawling on-premises data centre that required costly hardware refresh cycles every three years. With aging infrastructure, the engineering team spent 60% of their time on maintenance rather than innovation.

Their pain points:
- **$2.4M annual hardware & hosting costs**
- Lengthy provisioning times (4-6 weeks for new environments)
- Increasing compliance burden on self-managed infrastructure
- Limited disaster recovery capabilities

## Our Approach

We started with a 2-week discovery phase to catalogue all 47 workloads, assess their cloud-readiness, and produce a detailed Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) analysis.

### Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-3)
Set up AWS Landing Zone, identity federation, and network architecture using a hub-and-spoke VPC design.

### Phase 2: Pilot Migration (Weeks 4-7)
Migrated three non-critical workloads using the lift-and-shift approach to validate tooling and runbooks.

### Phase 3: Production Migration (Weeks 8-12)
Executed the remaining 44 workloads using a combination of:
- **Rehost** (lift & shift with CloudEndure) for legacy apps
- **Replatform** (RDS, ECS) for database and containerisable services
- **Refactor** (Lambda, API Gateway) for three customer-facing APIs
