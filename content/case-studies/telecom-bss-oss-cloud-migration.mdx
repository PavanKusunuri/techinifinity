## Challenge

TelecomOne’s billing and service orchestration stacks were tightly coupled to aging infrastructure, creating release bottlenecks and operational risk.

## Constraints

- Strict uptime requirements for charging and mediation
- Regulatory data retention and audit requirements
- Complex integrations with legacy network systems

## Migration Strategy

### Phase 1: Readiness & Risk Modeling
Created workload dependency maps and established blue/green deployment runbooks for critical components.

### Phase 2: Parallel Runtime
Ran legacy and cloud environments in controlled parallel mode with deterministic reconciliation before traffic cutover.

### Phase 3: Progressive Cutover
Shifted traffic by customer segment and region with real-time rollback controls and SRE war-room oversight.

## Measurable Impact

| Metric | Before | After |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Billing Platform Incidents (quarterly) | 34 | 18 |
| Average Release Lead Time | 20 days | 7 days |
| Sev-1 Recovery Time | 95 mins | 28 mins |
| Infrastructure Cost | Baseline | -29% |

## Technology Stack

- Kubernetes on AWS
- Kafka and event mediation services
- PostgreSQL and managed data services
- Prometheus, Grafana, and OpenTelemetry
