## The Challenge

RetailOps operated separate systems for point-of-sale, warehouse management, and eCommerce. Inventory mismatches led to frequent order cancellations and frustrated customers.

Key issues:
- Inconsistent stock visibility across channels
- Nightly batch sync delays causing stale data
- High manual reconciliation effort for store teams
- Lost revenue from avoidable out-of-stock events

## Our Approach

### Discovery & Domain Mapping (Weeks 1-3)
Mapped inventory-related business events and identified critical integration points across POS, ERP, and online storefronts.

### Event Platform Implementation (Weeks 4-9)
Built an event-driven integration layer with idempotent processing, schema validation, and replay capability for resilience.

### Store Rollout & Controls (Weeks 10-14)
Deployed phased rollout by region, introduced real-time monitoring dashboards, and trained operations teams.

## Results

| Metric | Before | After |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Inventory Accuracy | 76% | 97% |
| Stock-Out Incidents | Baseline | -31% |
| Order Cancellations (inventory mismatch) | 9.8% | 2.1% |
| Reconciliation Effort | 18 hrs/store/week | 6 hrs/store/week |

## Technology Stack

- AWS (EKS, MSK, Lambda, RDS)
- Event-driven architecture with Kafka
- Node.js and TypeScript microservices
- Datadog observability dashboards
